Hybrid Loop Comparison Lab

Compare direct control against two hybrid loops on the same bounded task. The lab separates three effects: pair acquisition, ambiguity removed before control, and the size of the remaining controller.

1. Raw family

The direct controller starts here, on all nonempty omission sets at once.

2. Pair acquisition

The hybrids first buy pair witnesses. This is the only up-front cost they pay.

3. Pure resolved mass

All non-singleton omissions are settled before the residual controller even begins.

4. Residual controller

The remaining controller acts only on singleton residue, either linearly or logarithmically.
